Visibility and compliance

Web Accessibility Audit and Fix

A full audit of your website against WCAG 2.1 AA, Australia's legal accessibility standard. We find every issue, fix it, and deliver a written report. Reduces your legal risk under the Disability Discrimination Act 1992 and improves your Google rankings.

From $400 one-off

Google Business Profile Setup and Optimisation

We claim and fully complete your Google listing including categories, description, photos, hours, services, and your direct review link. One of the highest-return single actions a local business can take.

From $250 one-off

Website Speed and Mobile Optimisation

We fix everything Google PageSpeed flags: compressing images, cleaning up code, and fixing mobile layouts. Faster sites rank higher and convert more visitors into customers. Includes a before and after score comparison.

From $300 one-off
