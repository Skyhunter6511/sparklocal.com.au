Visibility and compliance

Web Accessibility Audit and Fix

Australia's Disability Discrimination Act requires your website to be accessible to people with disabilities, and many businesses simply do not know this applies to them. We audit your site against WCAG 2.1 AA, fix every issue we find, and deliver a written report that doubles as a compliance record. As a bonus, accessible websites consistently rank higher in Google.

From $400 one-off

Google Business Profile Setup and Optimisation

Your Google Business listing is the first thing most locals see when they search for your service. We claim, complete, and optimise every field: categories, description, photos, hours, services, and your direct review link. One of the highest return on investment actions a local business can make.

From $250 one-off

Website Speed and Mobile Optimisation

A slow or mobile-unfriendly website quietly costs you customers every day. We streamline your site to load faster and behave beautifully on any device, improving both your Google ranking and the experience visitors have when they find you. Includes a clear before and after performance comparison.

From $300 one-off

Websites and conversion

Website Build

A clean, professional website built from the ground up. Whether you have no website at all or need to replace an outdated one, we design and build something that looks great, loads fast, works perfectly on mobile, and is accessible from day one. Packages range from a simple single-page presence to a full multi-page site depending on your needs.

From $400 one-off

Landing Page Build

When you are running ads, where you send that traffic matters enormously. A dedicated landing page with a single focused goal dramatically outperforms sending visitors to your homepage. We write all the copy, build the page, and set up conversion tracking so you can see exactly how many leads the page generates.

From $500 one-off

Photography Coordination

Great photography is one of the fastest ways to build trust online, and most local business websites are let down by poor quality images. We research and brief a local photographer, manage the shoot, review the results, and get everything uploaded to the right places, so you end up with photos that actually do your business justice.

From $200 plus photographer cost

From $400 per month

TikTok and Instagram Reels

Short-form video is the highest organic reach format available right now, and most local businesses are missing out entirely. You film on your phone, we handle scripting, editing, captions, music, and publishing, turning your raw footage into polished content that reaches new customers every week.

Ad spend paid directly to Google and separate from this fee.

Search and retention

Local SEO

Appearing in Google search for terms like "plumber Geelong" or "cafe Fitzroy" does not happen by accident. It requires consistent behind-the-scenes work: keyword research, on-page optimisation, citation building across Australian directories, and steady improvements month after month. Results compound over time and become one of your most valuable sources of new customers.

From $500 per month

Email Newsletter

Your existing customers are your most valuable audience, and a well-written monthly email is one of the most cost-effective ways to keep them engaged. We handle everything from content planning and copywriting through to design and sending, using your subscriber list and your brand.

From $200 per month

Google Review Strategy

More five-star reviews means a higher ranking in local search and more trust from new customers. We build a complete system for consistently generating reviews: follow-up SMS templates, a branded QR code, and monthly management of all responses on your behalf, positive and negative alike.

From $150 per month

Monthly Reporting Dashboard

A clear, plain-English report covering website traffic, Google ranking changes, social media growth, and ad performance. From $100 per month
