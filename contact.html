Common questions

Things people often ask

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

No. All monthly services are month-to-month with 30 days notice to cancel. We do ask you to sign a service agreement before we start, but that is just to protect both of us by clearly defining what has been agreed.

How quickly will I see results?

It depends on the service. Google Business optimisation can show results within days. Paid ads can generate leads within the first week. Social media builds over 2 to 3 months. SEO typically takes 3 to 6 months before significant ranking changes. This is just how Google works and we will always set honest expectations upfront.

Can you guarantee results?

No ethical agency can guarantee specific rankings or revenue, and any agency that does is not being straight with you. What we can guarantee is that we will do the right work, measure everything, report honestly, and adjust based on real data every month.

My website is on Wix or Squarespace. Can you still help?

Absolutely. We work with all the major website builders as well as HTML sites. For accessibility and speed work on builders like Wix and Squarespace, there are some platform limitations we cannot control, but we will always tell you clearly what can and cannot be fixed.

I am not sure which service I need. Can you help me figure it out?

That is exactly what the free consultation is for. Send us a message telling us a bit about your business and what you are hoping to achieve, and we will recommend the best starting point, no charge and no pressure.
