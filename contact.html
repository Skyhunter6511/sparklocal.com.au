Common questions

Things people often ask

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

No — all monthly services are month-to-month with 30 days notice to cancel. We do ask you to sign a service agreement before we start, but that's just to protect both of us by clearly defining what's been agreed.

How quickly will I see results?

It depends on the service. Google Business optimisation can show results within days. Paid ads can generate leads within the first week. Social media builds over 2–3 months. SEO typically takes 3–6 months before significant ranking changes — this is just how Google works, and we'll always set honest expectations upfront.

Can you guarantee results?

No ethical agency can guarantee specific rankings or revenue — and any agency that does is not being straight with you. What we can guarantee is: we'll do the right work, measure everything, report honestly, and adjust based on real data every month.

My website is on Wix or Squarespace — can you still help?

Absolutely. We work with all the major website builders as well as HTML sites. For accessibility and speed work on builders like Wix and Squarespace, there are some platform limitations we can't control — but we'll always tell you clearly what can and can't be fixed.

I'm not sure which service I need — can you help me figure it out?

That's exactly what the free consultation is for. Send us a message telling us a bit about your business and what you're hoping to achieve, and we'll recommend the best starting point — no charge, no pressure.
