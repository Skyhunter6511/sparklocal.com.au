Legal & compliance

Did you know your website has legal accessibility obligations?

Under Australia's Disability Discrimination Act 1992 — updated by the Australian Human Rights Commission in April 2025 — all Australian businesses must make their websites accessible to people with disabilities. This applies to your business whether you are a sole trader or a company.

Spark Local is one of the very few Victorian agencies that makes accessibility a core service. We audit and fix your website against the WCAG 2.1 AA standard — reducing your legal exposure while improving your Google search rankings at the same time.
