Legal and compliance

Did you know your website has legal accessibility obligations?

Under Australia's Disability Discrimination Act 1992 , all Australian businesses must make their websites accessible to people with disabilities. The Australian Human Rights Commission updated its guidance on this in April 2025, explicitly covering websites, mobile apps, and email communications for all businesses, whether you are a sole trader or a large company.

Spark Local is one of the few Victorian agencies that treats accessibility as a core service. We audit and fix your website against the WCAG 2.1 AA standard, reducing your legal exposure while improving your Google search rankings at the same time.
